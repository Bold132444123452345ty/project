Who we are

Our Achievements

The idea of having KVTC was conceived in the mind of management in 2017. At this point, the secondary school had graduated two lots of form four classes. Management was interested in knowing where the candidates have gone after their high school education. It was realized that a few of them had placement in universities and tertiary institutions but a majority of them were still at home. It became necessary to have an absorption mechanism for the students who have completed their form four level, hence the vocational centre came into place.

We have graduated 65 students. Upon doing tracing, we are pleased that our graduates are in various establishments as employees. Some have ventured into business and they are making a mark. In our testimonials section, you can read of our stories of change.

Given the fact that our fee is the most affordable around, it gives us an edge. It allows us to meet our goal of uplifting students who were having difficulties in affording tertiary education. These students now have a place to hone their skills and be productive for self and country. We are in tandem with the vision 2030.
